"Nesos created the ideal environment in which Asep Srl, could launch a project consisting of educational activities and sport courses that could be attended comfortably from home. This has allowed to continue commercial activity during the pandemic while creating new opportunities for expansion disregarding geographical boundaries offering a service that eliminates distances for a better experience tailor made for our clients of all ages"

Massimiliano Gandini President-A.SE.P. Srl

"Arcos has continued to pursue a process of technological innovation by researching and developing new technical know-how. Thanks to our partners we have completed a project called Nesos, an e-commerce platform of management and delivery of online training courses. The objective is to offer to our clients a 360° experience, where productivity, simplicity and functionality are the keys. We wanted to create the better online learning experience possible by means of a tool equipped for any necessity. We realized a videoconference environment that could cancel distances, where sharing and synergy are the winning ingredients. Our clients are the heart of Nesos and our closeness to them enables us to create tailor-made features guaranteeing effective and constant support."

Andrea Bottaro President-Arcos Soc. Coop. Soc.
