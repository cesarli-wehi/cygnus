We’re passionate software engineers with a desire to create technology that will be worth of the future.

Weather it is software development or IT infrastructure designs, we have found our passion in making useful, solid, well engineered tech products that will survive the test of time.

WE BELIEVE IN GREAT IDEAS

We are expert developers and tech enthusiasts that find nothing more exciting than finding a great idea and pursue it until it is realised and successful.

That is how Nesos LMS was born, we saw an opportunity to change the remote learning landscape and decided to pursue it.

Now we provide private educators a way to cut costs, reach a new audience and make their business future-proof by removing geographical and physical obstacles between them and their audience. The goal is to give anyone who has something to teach the tools to reach anyone who has something to learn.
